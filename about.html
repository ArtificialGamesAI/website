Mark Sorrell founded Artificial Games in April 2026.

Artificial Games is a house for artificial games. The first work is a PoC called The Risk, in which AI characters are briefed by humans to play a politically charged game of territory and armies autonomously. Subsequent works will sit in the same interaction pattern: brief, watch, react, refine.

He has been writing about the idea behind this company since 2011. His recent writing can be found at marksorrell.substack.com.
